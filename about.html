Brief Music History

Music is already been part of our lives. Music thrives through changes in civilization and continues to contribute to shape culture anywhere in the world over time. Even with pandemics such as COVID-19 that music is not played in public gatherings due to health concerns; when following and taking safety measures in place including playing music virtually and at a safe distance still allow people to relieve stress, anxiety and also get to moving when conducting physical activity like dance to improve wellness.

Types of Music


There are so many ways to appreciate different forms of music. Its style can depend on our mood, our needs, environment, lifestyle and personality to name a few.

In general, music includes Classical, World and Contemporary Music. Each has its own uses when we conduct daily activities.

Music and Wellness

Listening music is one the activities that gives different emotions. When we feel happy we often celebrate with music. When we feel lonely, we use music as a way to express feelings and move one. When we conduct challenging task, for example lifting weights at the house. We play music to lift our motivation and carry things we don't expect we can do. Music allows us to have creative outlet for self-care and show our care for others too. Music allows us to set our mood and relieve our stress and we can dance in the music too. According to the beat and the tempo, we can have different styles of dance.Listen, groove and relax with tunes and rhythm. Stay safe and thanks for reading the post.
