Benefits of Music

MusikJamz Inc has been blogging music events, developments and whereabouts since 1990. We started as a small group of musicians and artists having the similar interests of fluorishing the Arts in the community. We advocate music as a form of self identification, community engagement and a social cause. Rhythms and flow got out from radio, television, computer and now in smartphones.

Looking forward over 30 years of dedication, our passion and lifelong learning mindset continue to keep up with the changes... especially with the social and technological changes. Creativity is the goal we strive for. Always!

Throughout the years MusikJamz has evolved its practices and promote inclusion with people of different backgrounds. Throughout the day, our main goal is stand up for artists, musicians and the public who bring music and arts to life to contribute entertainment and lifelong memories we are welcome to have a conversation

We are more than welcome to present to you our information in the website. Enjoy!

Most Popular Music Instruments


Vocals

The simplest form of music. You can talk with rhythm and that is singing. Vocal cords produces voice and accompanying with instruments you can make different songs. Vocals are the most flexible as it can go to different music genres. Besides, singing with instruments, there are als form of vocals such Acapella, Rap and Beatboxing to name a few.


Source: Jake Barros

Guitar

This string instrument is simple accompanying with a hand and finger movements for the string to vibrate in the are, you can generate music. Guitar is made of wood and metal. Guitar has an extensive use in Folk, Country, Western, Rock and even Pop Music.


Source: Andy Guitar

Piano

Sophisticated instrument. It can take sometime to learn the instrument but after learning it, there is just this feeling that you can compose a song. When doing vocal training and rehearsal, piano is a good way to measure the key scale of rank the voices in high or low frequency. Piano has been used in many occasions, in church and in the house. Piano has been used in almost all genres of Music particularly Gospel, Jazz, Soul and Pop music.


Source: Francesco Parrino

Drum

Sound of a loud beat excites, amplifies the song. Drum sound provides more interesting and technical depth in the song. Usually in tandem with other insturments suchas vocals, guitar and piano, drum can be done alone. It has been used for marches and to alert an announcement and now with the progress of technology, drum can be carried now in like a keyboard version. The alternative form of drum is drum machine. Drum has been extensively used in all forms of music and even with acoustic songs.


Source: LearnSomeDrums
